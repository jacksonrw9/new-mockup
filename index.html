Benefits of Digitized GMP Workflows

Ensure Data Integrity

Our software-based solution guides your data directly from your analytical instruments to your digital systems, removing human error from data capturing, formatting and entry processes.

Cost Savings

Eliminate paper printout expenses entirely. Reduce labor costs by decreasing deviation reports and automating data capture and manual formatting efforts. Keep your facility audit-ready at all times, minimizing potential down time and expenses of FDA audits.

Increased Lab Tech Productivity

Automating redundant and tedious tasks allows lab techs to focus on critical analysis, method optimization, and value-added laboratory activities.

Protection Against Data Loss

Continuous data capture is maintained even during network failures and external interruptions. Your critical analytical data is automatically preserved and synchronized once connectivity is restored.

Built-in Compliance

Built-in electronic review and signature workflows ensure 21 CFR Part 11 compliance for BioPharma manufacturers and Food Production facilities.

Enables Remote Operation

Not only do we digitize GMP workflows, our platform allows you to take samples remotely from our UI. Now your lab techs no longer need to be in a bunny suit to take scheduled or ad hoc samples.

The Technical Infrastructure That Makes All of This Possible:

Technical Architecture Diagram

Microservices Architecture:

That abstracts multi-vendor instruments so they appear as one instrument type with one interface on our UI. Meaning even if a customer has an assortment of Mettler Toledo, Cole Palmer and Thermo Scientific pH meters in their production facility, they all appear as a pH meter.

Secure MQTT Communication:

Our platform utilizes outbound only communication so there are no open ports or any way that someone can externally connect on your scientific instruments.

Server-Grade Connectivity on the IoT Edge:

critical instrument data and workflows stored on the Edge enabling the same continuous monitoring and batch sampling workflows even when the network goes out, and all of the data is stored there until the network is back up.

Prebuilt Device Agents:

Simply connect your scientific instruments – our custom-built device agents handle everything behind the scenes, automatically communicating in native protocols, capturing sample data, and feeding it to our Data Formatting Engine for standardization.
