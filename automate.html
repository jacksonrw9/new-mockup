Automating Data Formatting

Standardize Sample Results the Moment They're Captured with Phizzle's Data Formatting Engine

Our platform extracts data from thousands of GMP instruments, and then translates the multi-vendor results to a singular format that can be synced directly to LIMS, MES or QMS with zero manual touch points.

Request a Demo

How Phizzle's Data Formatting Engine Works:

Step 1:

The Edge Puck connects to your scientific instrument and captures their raw sample data using Phizzle's pre-built device agents.

Step 2:

Our Data Formatting Engine standardizes the captured data from multi-vendor instruments into a standardized format.

Step 3:

Formatted Data is stored and ready to be securely synced with digital systems once integration is complete.

Phizzle's Data Formatting Engine Enables:

Multi-Vendor Data Standardization

Most manufacturers have multi-vendor instruments for a single instrument type. Climet air particle counters and Beckman Coulter air particle counters and Lighthouse air particle counters all become one instrument. Your data formatting automatically converts all manufacturer-specific outputs that your LIMS, MES, or QMS can understand, regardless of instrument make or model.

Eliminates Manual Data Manipulation

No need to spend time reformatting Excel files, converting units, or restructuring data layouts to match your system requirements. Our Data Formatting Engine does all the tedious data manipulation automatically, freeing your team to focus on analysis instead of data prep.

Real-Time Data Processing

Data formatting happens the moment sample data is captured – There's no delay or batch processing. Your formatted data is ready to be sync to your digital systems immediately.

Configurable Output Formats

While we standardize the data structure, you maintain control over the specific format requirements for your systems – By configuring field mappings, unit conversions, and data layouts to match your exact LIMS, MES, or QMS specifications.
