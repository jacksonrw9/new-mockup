Phizzle Connected Plant: Enabling Paperless GMP Workflows

Transform paper-based GMP processes into efficient digitized workflows that integrate seamlessly with your existing SOPs and compliance parameters. From real-time monitoring and accelerating decision-making across manufacturing operations.

Request a Demo

Connected Plant is the only software-defined solution to automate data capture and enable remote operation of multi-vendor APCs, microbial air samplers, and other analytical scientific instruments in GMP/ISO environments – all from one UI.

How Our Platform Fits Into Pharma 4.0

Benefits of Connected Plant:

Ensures Data Integrity and Audit Readiness

Maintain the highest data integrity standards through automated capture, real-time formatting, and secure digital workflows with full E21 CFR Part 11. Automated documentation and compliance monitoring keep you continuously audit ready. When inspectors arrive, your data is organized and accessible.

Enables Remote Sampling from One Vendor Agnostic UI

Remote operation of APCs, microbial samplers, and analytical instruments from one vendor agnostic UI eliminates unnecessary cleanroom entries and reduces contamination risk. Lab technicians can schedule and monitor operations remotely, maintaining continuous control without being physically present for routine data collection, critical analysis, method development, and value-added activities instead of routine data collection.

Delivers Significant Cost Savings

Eliminating manual data capture and formatting removes paper costs and substantially reduces the number of deviation reports, lowering labor costs.
