About Phizzle

Transforming Manufacturing Through Intelligent Automation.

Our mission is to accelerate innovation by digitizing paper-based processes in regulated manufacturing environments.

We believe that in an era of advanced digital systems, no manufacturer should still rely on pen-and-paper data collection, manual transcription, or disconnected instruments that create data silos. Our platform, Connected Plant, bridges the gap between legacy analytical instruments and modern digital infrastructure. By connecting, automating, and integrating existing GMP workflows, we enable manufacturers to maintain the highest standards of data integrity while accelerating decision-making and reducing operational costs.
