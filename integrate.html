How Phizzle Integrates Data Sources with Digital Systems:

Integration Workflow Diagram

Step 1: Connecting

The Edge Puck connects to analytical scientific instruments and captures the raw sample data. Treatment from one UI – all with one paper trail.

Step 2: Automating

Connected Plant standardizes the data using Phizzle's Data Formatting Engine, and enables operators to review results with compliant e-signatures and maintain detailed audit logs for FDA readiness from one UI.

Step 3: Integrating

Once Connected Plant is integrated into a manufacturer's infrastructure, formatted data is securely synced to their digital systems (adhering to the existing SOPs and compliance parameters).
