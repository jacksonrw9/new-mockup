A self-contained, cleanable gateway with a built-in computer that connects analytical scientific instruments to your digital systems while meeting strict cleanroom standards — something no other device could deliver until now.

Step 1: Connecting

The Edge Puck connects to analytical scientific instruments and captures the raw sample data.

Step 2: Automating

Connected Plant standardizes the data using Phizzle's Data Formatting Engine, and enables operators to review results with compliant e-signatures and maintain detailed audit logs for FDA readiness from one UI – all with zero paper trail.

Step 3: Integrating

Once Connected Plant is integrated into a manufacturer's infrastructure, formatted data is securely synced to their digital systems (adhering to the existing SOPs and compliance parameters).

Benefits of the Edge Puck:

Versatile Connectivity

Allows lab techs to push a cart with an APC or a microbial air sampler and take samples throughout a facility while maintaining connectivity. Solves connectivity challenges in cramped cleanroom spaces where conventional gateways are too large or impractical to install.

Secures Vulnerable Analytical Instruments

Analytical instruments often use exposed communication protocols that create security vulnerabilities on your network. Phizzle's Edge Puck acts as a security barrier, converting vulnerable protocols like Modbus into encrypted, secure communications with MQTT. Eliminating direct network access to your instruments while maintaining full connectivity to your backend systems.

Malware Hardened

The Edge Puck's Read-Only File System provides inherent malware protection – since nothing can be written to the operating system, malicious software simply cannot install or execute.
